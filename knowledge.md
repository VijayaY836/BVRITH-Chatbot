## 1. About BVRITH

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH) is an autonomous engineering college established in 2012, the youngest institution under the Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES), founded by Chairman Sri K V Vishnu Raju.

The college was accredited by NAAC with Grade 'A' in 2020, and granted UGC Autonomous status from Academic Year 2023-24 for a period of 10 years. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), recognized by the Government of Telangana, and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).

BVRITH offers 4 UG programs (B.Tech in EEE, ECE, CSE, and CSE with Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) with a combined intake of 660 seats, and 3 PG programs: M.Tech in Data Sciences (intake 18), M.Tech in VLSI Design (intake 12), and M.Tech in CSE (intake 12).

### Accreditations

- NAAC: Grade 'A' (CGPA 3.23), awarded 2020
- NBA: EEE, ECE, and CSE branches are NBA-accredited (all 4 branches — CSE, ECE, IT & EEE — were NBA accredited in 2018)
- UGC Autonomous status conferred from AY 2023-24 for 10 years
- Ranked by NIRF for three consecutive years: 2019, 2020, and 2021
- Ranked by ARIIA-2020 in Band B (26th to 50th)

### Vision

To emerge as the best among the institutes of technology and research in the country dedicated to the cause of promoting quality technical education.

### Mission

- Achieve academic excellence through innovative learning practices.
- Enhance intellectual ability and technical competency for a successful career.
- Encourage research and innovation.
- Nurture students towards holistic development with emphasis on leadership skills, life skills, and human values.

### Notable Achievements

- Entered the Asia Book of Records (2018) for the largest Mental Health Peer Counseling event for Women.
- First college to initiate mentorship programs with top MNCs including Microsoft, Amazon, and Qualcomm.
- Received two JNTUH University Gold Medals across the first three graduating batches.
- Highest placement package on record: Microsoft at Rs. 54 LPA (2021-2025 batch).

### Campus Group

BVRITH is part of the Sri Vishnu Educational Society, which also operates: Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women, Vishnu Institute of Technology, B V Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT), Vishnu Dental College & Hospital, Shri Vishnu College of Pharmacy, and several other institutions across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. BVRITH itself is located in the 'Valley Vista' campus group in Hyderabad.

## 3. Admissions

### Eligibility

- 10+2 with MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) as major subjects.
- Minimum 60% marks.
- Selection per TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test) criteria.

### Entrance Exams & Seat Categories

Category A seats are filled by the Convener, TS EAMCET Admissions. Category B seats are filled directly by the college as per Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) guidelines.

College codes: EAMCET/ECET code BVRW, PGECET code BVRW1.

### Admission Process

- Applications open after TSEAMCET releases its notification for Convener seats, and after TSCHE releases its notification for B-category seats.
- Category A applications are submitted online through the TSEAMCET Convener portal.
- Category B applications are submitted online through the college website.
- A complete admissions schedule is published by TSCHE each cycle; re-application is not required once submitted.

For admissions queries, the college lists a dedicated contact: Dr J. Manoj Kumar, Professor i/c Admissions, 92471 64714.

## 6. Campus & Facilities

### Library

The BVRITH Library is housed in a four-storey building known as the Ruby Block. It maintains a collection of textbooks, reference books, novels, encyclopaedias, magazines, and national/international journals.

- Library hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Issue & Return counter hours: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
- Member of DELNET and the National Digital Library of India (NDLI).
- Catalogued using the Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC) system.
- Digital library: 10 computers with internet access, NPTEL video lectures, and online mock tests.
- E-journal access via IEEE-ASPP and J-GATE (JET) through the KNIMBUS mLibrary platform, including remote/mobile access.
- Plagiarism checking via iThenticate (Turnitin) for staff and student publications and final-year projects.
- Floor layout: Ground floor — digital library, periodicals, newspapers, reading area. First floor — CSE, IT & BS&H stacks. Second floor — ECE & EEE stacks. Third floor — reference section, project reports, activity area.
- Literary club: 'PORTIA: The Literary Coven', run through the library.
- Rare book collection dating from 1919 onwards.

Library staff: Dr. S. Satya Jaya (Librarian, M.Com, M.L.I.Sc, PhD); Y. Renuka, G. Krishnaveni, and G. Urmilasree (Library Assistants).

### Hostel

Hostel buildings are located within the college campus for security and convenience. The hostel comprises 4 blocks with 150+ rooms and an occupancy of 500+ students.

- Lady wardens and dedicated security officers are assigned to each block.
- 100% ragging-free, with an active anti-ragging squad.
- A full-time lady physical director organizes outdoor games (volleyball, badminton, throw-ball) and fitness activities.
- Amenities: 24/7 water supply, geyser systems, nutritious food, common room, gym area, television/entertainment access.
- Additional highlights listed by the college: fresh-choice bakery, fast-food center, yoga & sports facilities, eco-friendly surroundings.
