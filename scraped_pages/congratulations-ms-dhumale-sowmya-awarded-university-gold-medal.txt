Dhumale Sowmya(14WH1A0231) of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women, Bachupally has been awarded University Gold Medal for having secured first rank in B.Tech (Electrical & Electronics Engineering) from all JNTUH Affiliated Colleges amongst all the graduates of the academic year 2017-18. She has been awarded from  JNTUH University  at VIII Convocation on 26th April 2019.
