# Aspiring Minds’ National Employability Award 2017
Source: https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/national-employability-award17

Post author: bVR1t-Wom3n
Post published: March 12, 2016
Post category: Awards
“ Congratulations! It is our pleasure to inform you that as per the AMCAT scores of your students of 2017 batch, you have been chosen for the National Employability Award. You are one of the 18 campuses who have been awarded with this unique award. This award states in no uncertain terms that your campus is one of the top 10% engineering campuses in Telangana. We congratulate your continuous efforts in enhancing the employability of your students and bringing them to industry standards. ”
New Delhi, February 1, 2017 : Aspiring Minds has announced the third list of colleges to be awarded the National Employability Award 2017 for being among the top 10% engineering campuses nationally or in their respective states.
The awards started in 2012 by Aspiring Minds, are the only awards given on the basis of actual performance of students on a standardized test and not on the basis of superficial factors like infrastructure, faculty etc. Through these awards, Aspiring Minds felicitates colleges that are in the lead on the basis of scores of their final year students on AMCAT, India’s largest employability test.
